      💡 [How to Interpret These Values]
      The calculated amount represents the "suggested risk tolerance (maximum acceptable loss) per single trade" based on historical data, and does NOT represent the total position size. This is purely a mathematical model (the Kelly Criterion) intended to help manage capital volatility in systematic trading, and it does not guarantee future profits. Please use it strictly as a reference and execute trades at your own discretion, taking into account the risk of sudden market changes.
